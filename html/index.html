About Me

Hi welcome! I'm Joaquin Pacia, a 20-year-old Filipino-Canadian.

I am currently enrolled in the TCS + CCIT programs at the University of Toronto Mississauga. My interests are far-ranging and eclectic. I'm an aspiring multimedia designer, programmer, and web developer, with experience in live-stream production. I love technology and enjoy gaming especially simulation games, base-building games, and co-op games. When I'm not at my computer, I'm likely to be found playing spikeball, badminton, or table tennis. I'm always eager to learn and grow in my areas of interest and look forward to the journey ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

My key areas of expertise lie in graphic design, video editing, and live-stream production. I'm constantly honing my skills in these areas and exploring new techniques and tools.

I was drawn to graphic design and video editing because of their blend of creativity and technicality. I love the process of turning a concept into a visual reality and the problem-solving aspect of optimizing designs for different platforms and audiences.

My experience in live-stream production has been both challenging and rewarding. It requires a good understanding of both the technical aspect of managing the streaming equipment and the creative aspect of producing content that engages viewers.

Some of my favorite anime series are Attack on Titan, My Hero Academia, and Demon Slayer. I enjoy watching anime because of the unique stories and characters that are often thought-provoking and emotionally impactful.

My hobbies and interests feed directly into my academic and professional pursuits. For example, my love for technology and gaming has influenced my decision to study in the TCS + CCIT programs. My interest in graphic design and video editing shapes my perspective on different projects and assignments.

Balancing my time is a constant process. I prioritize my academic and professional responsibilities but also allocate time for my hobbies and personal interests. This balance helps me stay productive and also gives me time to relax, reflect, and enjoy life. It's all about setting priorities and being organized.

I chose the TCS + CCIT programs because they align with my passion for technology and creativity. The courses offer a mix of theoretical knowledge and practical skills that are directly applicable to my interests in gaming, graphic design, and video editing. It's the perfect fit for me.
